I am a PhD student in the economics department at Harvard University, a research affiliate at Opportunity Insights and EdRedesign, and a Stone Scholar in Inequality and Wealth Concentration at Harvard Kennedy School. My research is primiarly in public and labor economics with a focus on race- and class-based economic disparities in the US. I study the sources of these disparities and evaluate policies that aim to alleviate them. I will be on the job market in fall 2023.
