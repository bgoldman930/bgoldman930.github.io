I am a PhD student in the economics department at Harvard University, a research affiliate at Opportunity Insights and EdRedesign, and a Stone Scholar in Inequality and Wealth Concentration at Harvard Kennedy School. My research is primarily in labor and public economics with a focus on race- and class-based economic disparities in the US. I study the sources of these disparities and evaluate policies that aim to alleviate them.

Beginning in July, I will be an Assistant Professor in the Department of Economics and the Jeb E. Brooks School of Public Policy at Cornell University.
