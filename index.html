I am a PhD student in the economics department at Harvard University, a research affiliate at Opportunity Insights and EdRedesign, and a Stone Scholar in Inequality and Wealth Concentration at Harvard Kennedy School. My research is primarily in public and labor economics with a focus on race- and class-based economic disparities in the US. I study the sources of these disparities and evaluate policies that aim to alleviate them.

I will be on the job market during the 2023-2024 academic year. My contact information, references, and job market paper are on my job market page.
