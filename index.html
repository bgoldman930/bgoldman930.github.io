I am a labor and public economist studying economic disparities by class and race in the United States. Much of my work examines the role of family formation in shaping these disparities. I hold appointments at:

  • Cornell University — Assistant Professor, Economics & Public Policy
  • NBER — Faculty Research Fellow
  • Opportunity Insights — Research Principal

I received my PhD in Economics from Harvard University in 2024. I coach quarterbacks for the Ithaca High School football team.
